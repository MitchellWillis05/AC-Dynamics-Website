Meet The Team

I'm Jon Laheney, the owner of AC Dynamics.

I have been working in the industry for over 20 years with extensive experience in the Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration industry.

I started my trade in the Navy as a Marine Engineer Mechanic, servicing and maintaining industrial marine-grade refrigeration systems of all ages, shapes, and sizes. I progressed through the ranks until it was time to leave and advance my career on land.

Travelling around the UK’s major cities and working in hospitals, office high-rises, banks, supermarkets, data centres, factories, aquariums, universities, government and military facilities, with the occasional historical buildings to name but a few, I gained a wealth of knowledge and experience in installing and servicing various refrigeration plants and HVAC systems.

After visiting New Zealand, I fell in love with the place and moved here. I’ve been here ever since. Having gained extensive experience in air conditioning, I was able to establish a solid foundation to build AC Dynamics.
